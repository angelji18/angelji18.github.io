Who I am

Animation and Gaming Enthusiast

Smooth flowing movements in animation, and special effects that go with it can make or break my day.

Personally, I enjoy coding in C++ as I feel it’s more customizable and gives me better control. However, I have worked extensively with Python, C# and even Java.

In my Graduate studies, I’ve had the chance to venture into animation and game development. I made a short animation film using C++ and SDL2 and have also developed a fighting-sidescroller-game of my own. I also successfully created a virtual replica of an educational-hex hardware object using Unity and C#. This Unity based hex object educates the masses on the issue of Female Feticide with the help of real-time movement and animation using user key presses and color changing features, all to represent its real-life hardware implementation. In the coming semesters I’ll also be working in Unity to implement Virtual Reality in gaming for which I’m excited.
